# Vision

## DroidPuppy

**DroidPuppy is an Android-native agent operating layer.**

That means more than "mobile support" or "Android compatibility."
It means building a real environment where agents can operate naturally on a phone.

## What that means in practice

DroidPuppy should help agents:

- launch and route between Android apps
- open and navigate system settings
- work through browser flows on-device
- use wireless ADB and CDP when deeper browser control is available
- hand work across Android-native surfaces like browsers, share flows, and device tools
- prefer simple, human-friendly commands over low-level implementation details

## The direction

DroidPuppy is meant to grow into a layered Android system for agents:

1. **Core Android utilities**
2. **Browser launch and control**
3. **Wireless ADB / CDP bridging**
4. **Friendly read and action helpers**
5. **Workflow orchestration and specialized Android agents**

## Why it matters

Phones are not just small desktops.
They are their own environment with their own strengths, constraints, and interaction patterns.

DroidPuppy exists to make agents feel native there.
